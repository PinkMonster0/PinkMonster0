Translucent Object Rendering

Made by Zhang Jiajian and Zou Muyang.

This is the course project of Computer Graphic I. Our target is to render translucent obeject with the method of BSSRDF(Bidirectional scattering distribution function).

In reproducing the origin paper of BSSRDF (https://graphics.stanford.edu/papers/bssrdf/bssrdf.pdf), I simplified some distribution function to get a result close to the origin effect.

And Zhang Jiajian used an Nvidia's framework OptiX, it helped to easily get parallel calulation in ray tracing process. so our render can be done in even real-time.
