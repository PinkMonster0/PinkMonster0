Illusion

This project is created by me and He Jiayu(a teammate in Photon). We want to test the sketch rendering effect and create a game with a rhythmic and flexible playing experience.

The mini game tells a very simple story. A little girl swim in her dream. We designed the motion and levels according to the rhythmic sound effect (waves and breathing). I think it has particular pleasure in relaxed experience.
