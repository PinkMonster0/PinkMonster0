Illusion

Produced by He Jiayu and Zou Muyang

This project is created by me and He Jiayu(a teammate in Photon). We want to test the sketch rendering effect and create a game with a rhythmic and flexible playing experience.

The mini game tells a very simple story: A little girl swim in her dream. We designed the motions and levels according to the rhythmic sound effects (waves and breathing). I think it has specially relaxing pleasure.

The purpose of this project is helping He Jiayu to apply master program in game design. So he has done the mainly design of art resource and story. I do the level design and adjustment work for balancing motions, sounds, and emotion flow.

Full game package available upon request.
