Image Cartoonlization

It's a big group project. Our members' list can be seen in the report at the end of introduction.

This is the course project of Computer Vision I (which a took in sophomore)

Inspired by the paper of Cartoon GAN, we try to understand and reproduce the Cartoonlization effect. (https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content_cvpr_2018/papers/Chen_CartoonGAN_Generative_Adversarial_CVPR_2018_paper.pdf)

We trained the network by our own database, but the effect is not very good. After we change the loss function and do some pre-operation to training data(weaken highlight).We got a reasonable result.

My contributions are making data set and implementing highlight softing pre-operation.
