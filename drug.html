Drug

This game was designed and produced in 72 hours in NetEase gamejam. The theme is 'medicine', we didn't consider how to make the game at the beginning. We just thought that we can't fight with bacterial directly, it's our immune cells and the medicine we took helped use fight with bacterial. So, this is how we get the idea of this rare RTS play mode.

From two sides of the screen, the immune cells and bacterial will be generated automatically and randomly. We intentionally adapt the generation rate, so the bacterial will win if the player don't do anything. And we can use different types of drugs to help immun cells defeat bacterial.

Different with the style of Inkun, we choose bright and high contrast color in this game. I think it's also impactful. We designed three levels corresponding three organs in human body. And we optimize the UI after the match.
