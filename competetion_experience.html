Competetion Experiences

During the college time, I actively participated in the contests like hackthons and gamejams with my friends. These experiences not only let me learn more skills, but also helped me realize my interest.

In Nov. 2017, I teamed up with my four friends to participated in Accenture Digital Global Connected Hackathon.In this 24-hour Hackthon, participants need to make a series of concepts and prototypes of new products and services, and show certain social and economic values of them. For example, how does digital technology change urban life? For another example, when we re-examine multiple values, how can digital technology make the social environment more inclusive? Or how does digital technology change the way we interact with the outside world?

We proposed and designed an travel app demo 'Passoul'. 'Passoul' is designed for youth group with sentimental emotion, and aims to create surprises for users during the journey. The application uses technologies such as geographic location-based services, big data, and machine learning to personalize the scenery during the trip according to the user's preferences. If the user is a movie enthusiast, the app will tell him that this was the location of a classic movie; if the user likes history, the recommended message will be based on the history of the place.

Another feature of this app is that it can gather users with 'empathy'. When a user learns about a certain scenic spot through the application, he/she can see the messages of users who have the same preferences as him/her on the personalized page, and can interact with each other. In the end, we won the first prize by this app demo. For me, it's the first successful experience to cooperate with teammates to propose and refine ideas, produce demo and give a public presentation.
